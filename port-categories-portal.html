The Three Port Categories

Port numbers are divided into three official ranges by IANA — the Internet Assigned Numbers Authority.

🔒
Well-Known Ports
0 – 1,023
Reserved for major, globally recognized protocols. Standardized worldwide.
Like emergency numbers — 911, 311. Everyone knows them. They never change.
📋
Registered Ports
1,024 – 49,151
Assigned to specific applications and companies by IANA on request.
Like business phone extensions — officially documented so no two companies share the same one.
🔓
Dynamic / Ephemeral
49,152 – 65,535
Temporary ports assigned automatically by the OS for client-side sessions.
Like visitor badges — assigned when you arrive, returned when the session ends.
